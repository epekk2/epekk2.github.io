Extroversion

Enthusiasm - Assertiveness

This trait is essentially how energetic, sociable, and friendly someone is

When you refer to someone as a 'people person', you are really saying they are high in extroversion

Take this quiz to see if you're an extroverted person or not!

If you are extroverted you are more likely to: gain energy from social situations, be assertive, talkative, action-oriented, like being the center of attention, usually have a good outlook on life

If you are more introverted you are more likely to: prefer spending more time alone as you don't require as much social stimulation to be content, have a small friend group, be very self-aware, be thought of as quiet by others
