Conscientiousness

Responsibility - Orderliness

This trait is essentially how self-aware one is. They tend to be responsible, goal oriented, and industrious.

They are likely to have high self-control, be reliable, and is a predictor for lifetime success

Take this quiz to see if you're a conscientious person or not!

If you are conscientious you are more likely to: Be skilled at running a company, struggle with ODC or perfectionism (when scoring very highly), have principles you live your life by

If you are less conscientious you are more likely to: prioritize momentary fun, be thought of as irresponsible by friends, give into impulse, have a dirty/unorganized room
