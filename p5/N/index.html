Neuroticism

Withdrawal - Volatility

This trait is essentially how sensitive emotion one is

For example, someone scores high in neuroticism will respond much worse to stress than someone scores low

Take this quiz to see if you're a neurotic person or not!

If you are neurotic you are more likely to: get angry easily, have high anxiety, be quite self- conscious, be depressed, have an eating disorder, or see ordinary situations as threatening

If you are less neurotic you are more likely to: handle stress well, experience emotions such as sadness, fear, and guilt less often, can work for longer periods of time without being burnt out, can be too optimistic
