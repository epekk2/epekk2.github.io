Openness

Intellect - Creativity

This trait is essentially how open to new ideas one is. They seek out new and novel experiences

They are very curious about the world and are much more likely to be creative

Take this quiz to see if you're an open person or not!

If you are open you are more likely to: appreciate all kinds of arts, like many genres of music, have pretty fluid opinions, and are most likely a liberal.

If you are less open you are more likely to: like having a set routine each day, subscribe to tradition, dislike change due to enjoying familiarity, and are most likely a conservative
