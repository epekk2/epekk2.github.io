Agreeableness

Compassion - Politeness

This trait is essentially how understanding of others someone is

They are friendly, warm people that will always lend a hand

Take this quiz to see if you're an agreeable person or not!

If you are agreeable you are more likely to: put others issues above your own, help others in any way you can, have a good outlook on humans, have trust in others, and have lots of sympathy for others

If you are less agreeable you are more likely to: prioritize your own interests above others, may be thought of as unfriendly and uncooperative, be competitive, have issues in your relationships
